# 19th Anniversary Celebration

**Date:** June 14, 2026  
**Service:** Online Sunday Service — First Service  
**Speaker:** Bishop JB Masinde
**Theme:** Year of Double Harvest — Zechariah 9:12  

---

## Key Highlights

### Anniversary Celebration (38:07 - 39:51)
The congregation joyfully celebrated 19 years of ministry. The service included prayers for continued multiplication and growth based on Deuteronomy 11:21.

### The Holy Spirit as the Ultimate Upgrade (56:23)
The sermon focused on the Holy Spirit as a divine helper. The pastor explained that as believers face new seasons and challenges, they need a spiritual "upgrade"—much like software updates on a device—to function effectively.

### Developing Spiritual Intelligence
A core theme was the integration of natural skills with spiritual power. Four types of intelligence were identified:

- **Book Smarts** — Cognitive intelligence (e.g., Moses learned in Egyptian wisdom, Acts 7:22)
- **People Smarts** — Emotional intelligence for leadership and relationships
- **Money Smarts** — Financial intelligence for stewardship and navigating scarcity (Genesis 47:14-15)
- **Spiritual Intelligence** — The edge provided by the Holy Spirit and the nine gifts of the Spirit (1 Corinthians 12)

### The Gifts of the Spirit (1:11:22 - 1:20:44)
The pastor detailed the manifestations of the Spirit—word of wisdom, word of knowledge, faith, healing, and miracles—emphasizing that these gifts are given to every believer for the benefit of the body.

## Service Ceremonies

- **Child Dedication (2:25:24 - 2:30:53)** — Families and children dedicated to the Lord
- **Welcoming Visitors (2:33:42 - 2:37:23)** — First-time visitors warmly welcomed and prayed for

The service concluded with the congregation speaking Acts 20:32 as a prayer of blessing over one another.
